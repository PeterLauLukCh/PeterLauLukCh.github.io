Hi!

Peter Lau-Luk Chen


I am an undergradute student from Columbia, doing a double major in Mathematics and Computer Science. I am now working in Prof. Andrew Blumberg's group and also working with Prof. Tianyi Lin

My interests lies in the intersection of machine learning, optimization (non-convex & stochastic optimization, optimal transport), and topological/geometric data analysis, applying them to the areas such as vision, high-dimensional bio data, and LLMs.

Currently, my works are spanning on geometry processing, LLM preference optimization, and optimal transport.

I am also affiliated to Musketeers Foundation Institute of Data Science (IDS HK), and my research is partially supported by IDS research grant.
